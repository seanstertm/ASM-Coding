Slay the Spire is another roguelike, but this one is about turn based combat and a deck of cards. Every run you start with a deck of cards and add and remove cards over the course of your run. All four characters have different card sets each, which keeps the game very exciting.
It is a little too luck based for my liking, but I love the different levels of difficulty it has. My favorite character is the Defect, who uses orbs of energy to attack.
