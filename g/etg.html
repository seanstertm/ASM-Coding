Enter the Gungeon is a top down shooter, with cute graphics and funny guns. The game centers around using weapons and finding synergies between them. Every floor has a secret floor after it, leading to many paths to choose from, and with eight characters the game never gets old.
The game manages to be totally skill based for a very luck based genre. No run will be ridiculously bad or ridiculously good, since the good guns have less ammo, and the bad guns spawn less often, and the loot has many systems to guarantee you get what you need while still being random.
