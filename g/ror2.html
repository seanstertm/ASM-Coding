And for the final game, another roguelike. This one is a 3d 3rd person shooter with constantly spawning enemies on planets. It is very standard and basic, as far as roguelikes go, but there isn't much glaringly bad about it.
Where this game shines is in its co-op mode. Everything you can do alone, you can do with a friend, and it's even more fun. There isn't much variety in gameplay, but it still is good with someone else.
