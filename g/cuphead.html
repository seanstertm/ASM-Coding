Cuphead is a sidescrolling shooting game full of boss fights. The whole game gives off the vibe of an old cartoon, and is absolutely beautiful in my opinion. It is notoriously difficult, and very hard to master.
This game is also extremely fun in multiplayer. My friend and I played through a lot of it, and we had a ton of fun. Cuphead is expecting DLC in the summer, which I am very excited for.
