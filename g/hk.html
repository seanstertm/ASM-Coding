Hollow knight is an open world metrovania with the best soundtrack of any game I've ever played. You play as a little bug investigating a ruined city, and gain abilities along the way. The game is quite difficult, but can appeal to many different ways of playing.
I personally have gotten in to speedrunning this game, and the replayability is off the charts, even though it isn't a roguelike like most of my favorite games. Hollow Knight has an anticipated sequel coming out... eventually. It was supposed to release a while ago, but news about it has been sparce.
