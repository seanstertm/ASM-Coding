Revita is roguelike combination of Hollow Knight and Isaac, but with a gun. The bosses, levels, and areas are quite well done, with an overall feel of being very pretty. The game isn't suuuper diverse in terms of items and paths, but everything else makes up for it tremendously.
Just coming out of early access as of a few weeks ago, Revita is a relatively new game. The community is great, and the developer is very active in the community. There is hardly any wiki for it yet, which is slightly disappointing but I'm sure that will grow over time.
