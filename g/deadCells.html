Yet another roguelike, Dead Cells is a game where you run around smacking enemies and upgrading your stats and gear. It is 2d, and has hundreds of routes to choose from. The constant updates and DLC make this game stand out, and I've been following it for years.
The recent update adds a new final boss, and polishes up the game. I've only been enjoying it more and more as time goes on, and I hope to some day get past the 3rd of 5 difficulties.
