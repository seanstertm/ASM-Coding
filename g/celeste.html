Celeste is quite possibly one of the hardest games ever. It was also the first game I got 100% on steam for so it holds a special place in my heart. It is an 8 bit platforming game, with a huge learning curve and fun mechanics.
Originally, the game had 8 chapters with 3 difficulties, and I averaged a couple hundred deaths on the hardest difficulty. After the release of the Chapter 9 DLC, I had 3000 deaths on that level alone. That level is one of the best experiences gaming I've ever had.
